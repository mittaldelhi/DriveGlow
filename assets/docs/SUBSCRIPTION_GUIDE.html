📋 Understanding Subscription Plans

🌟 Premium Wash Plan

  • Duration: 1 Month (30 days)
  • Max Services: 4 per month
  • Price: ₹999/month
  • Included Services: Exterior Wash, Interior Vacuum, Tire Shine, Dashboard Wipe
  • Daily Limit: 1 free service per day

Key Points About Plans:

  • Duration = How long the plan is valid (30, 90, or 365 days)
  • Max Services = Total services you can use
  • Included Services = Only these services are FREE
  • Daily Limit = Only 1 free service per day per vehicle

Example: Can I get free service for non-subscribed service?

No! If Raj has Premium Plan but wants "Deep Wash" (not in plan), he must pay full price. Subscription only covers specific listed services.

⏰ Daily Limit Rule

The Golden Rule: ONE Service Per Day Per Vehicle

Once you book any subscription service for a vehicle, you CANNOT book another subscription service for that same vehicle until the next day - even if you cancel.

Example - What Happens:

Time Action Result
Day 1, 9 AM Book Exterior Wash ✅ Success
Day 1, 2 PM Book Interior Vacuum ❌ BLOCKED
Day 1, 4 PM Cancel 9 AM booking ✅ Cancelled
Day 1, 5 PM Book Interior Vacuum ❌ Still BLOCKED
Day 2 Book Interior Vacuum ✅ Success!
Why can't I re-book after cancelling?
Because you already consumed the daily slot by making the first booking. Cancellation doesn't restore it.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Can I cancel my subscription plan?
❌ NO. Only Admin can cancel subscription plans. You cannot cancel it yourself. You must wait until it expires naturally or contact support.
Q2: Can I cancel a subscription service booking?
✅ YES. You can cancel your service bookings, but: The service still counts as "used", you cannot book another service that day, and your remaining services do NOT increase.
Q3: I cancelled my booking. Why can't I book again today?
Because the daily limit is 1 per day per vehicle. Once you booked (even if you cancelled), that day's slot is consumed. Try again tomorrow.
Q4: Can I use subscription for any vehicle?
❌ NO. Each subscription is tied to ONE specific vehicle. The vehicle is locked when you purchase the plan.
Q5: Can my friend use my subscription?
❌ NO. Subscriptions are per-user, per-vehicle. Only the person who purchased can use it.
Q6: I want a service not in my plan. Can I get it free?
❌ NO. Only services listed in your plan are free. Other services cost full price.
Q7: What happens when my subscription expires?
The vehicle badge disappears, you cannot book free services, but you can purchase a new subscription. Existing bookings continue until completed.
Q8: Can I share one subscription between two vehicles?
❌ NO. Each vehicle needs its own subscription. They are vehicle-specific.
Q9: What if I miss my appointment (lapsed)?
The lapsed booking still counts as "used" against your plan. You lose that service opportunity.
Q10: Can I get a refund for unused services?
Contact Admin. User cancellations do not restore service counts.
Q11: Do regular paid services count toward subscription?
❌ NO. Paid regular services are completely separate. They do not affect your subscription usage or limits.
Q12: Can I book services for different vehicles on the same day?
✅ YES! Daily limit is per vehicle, not per user. You can book one service for Fortuner and one for Innova on the same day.
